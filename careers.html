Careers

ADCO CNC is a small company, currently expanding our staff and capabilities, but still with a friendly family-like environment. We are looking for motivated professionals for the following positions:

CNC Machinist / Operator

We are looking for qualified CNC Machinists who are able to do machining as well as setup and occasionally edit existing CNC programs. Must be able to work fast and with confidence, be effective and efficient.

Must have experience with:

  • HAAS Mill controls
  • HAAS Lathe controls
  • Reading of technical drawings and documents
  • Advanced measurement skills
  • Quality Assurance verification skills
  • Ability to work independently
  • Ability to set up working process
